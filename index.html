Activities


Sports is an important part of mental and physical wellness.That's why we provide a wide range of indoor and outdoor games.We also have annual sports day so students can display their sports skills in competions like football,cricket and inter-house races.


Our houses

To develop teamwork skills in children we have made four "houses" which are:Red(Arjuna),Yellow(Abhimanyu),Green(Eklavya),Blue(Karna).



Swimming

We have a semi-olympic swimming pool with each class a period once a week of swimming as it is an excellent exercise for muscles.We also have a baby pool for the pre-primary section.An "Aquatic Meet" is held every year so students can show their swimming talent.Our swimming instuctors and life guards are highly trained and their are seperate instuctors and changing rooms for boys and girls.





Our Playground

We have a large playground for sports like football,cricket etc.
We have a special tie-up with "Cricket Australia" for teaching our students in the Gentalman"s game.Legendry International cricketer from Australia Mr. Bret Lee visited our school on Monday, 28th July 2014 at Jaipur. The students had a healthy interaction and were given his useful tips.





Our art studio

Art and crafts are an important way to express creativity,that's why we have two fully equipped art studios which are:

Art Room:To incultivate the young Picasso's inside our students we a large Art Room.Children learn how to put their imaginations on paper.This encourages them to visualise whole new world in their mind.

Craft Room:An experienced and proffesional teacher imparts different skills to the students like making a paper lamp,pencil holder and much much more.Our Craft Room lets children explore their creativity and also teaches them how to be eco-friendly.
